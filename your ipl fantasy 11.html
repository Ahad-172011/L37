Create your playing XI



The IPL Fantasy League is a virtual game where participants create teams using real IPL players and earn points based on their on-field performances. Your goal? Outscore rivals by picking the right mix of batsmen, bowlers, all-rounders, and wicketkeepers within a budget. Platforms like Dream11, MyTeam11, and ESPNcricinfo host these leagues, offering cash prizes, merchandise, and eternal glory.
